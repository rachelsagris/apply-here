# Apply Here
If you're curious what modern job searching is like, or just want to know that you're not alone, this game might help. It includes most features of the modern job market — but also something "real life" may have forgotten: a reward for your disappointment, and a little ancient wisdom to keep you going when your professional hope runs out.
—
While the original content was written by numerous job recruiters and automated company emails, I paraphrased to protect their identity. Assistance with coding and "a few other things" was provided by AI. My husband wrote the content for the webinar graph 1 and chose the "theme song" for the lose ending. The game idea, structure, theme, and rewards are my own. Moral support and a few random keystrokes provided by Finn.
